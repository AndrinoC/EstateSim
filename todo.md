## EstateSim Ideas

1. Add stronger economic tradeoffs so the midgame is about choices, not just scaling production.
2. Give territories unique identities with local modifiers, faction behavior, and exclusive rewards.
3. Deepen combat with unit counters, supply, recovery, and different attack types like raids and sieges.
4. Expand population management with pressure systems such as happiness, stability, sanitation, or class demands.
5. Add more sinks for crafted goods through governance, diplomacy, prestige, and military progression.
6. Make the market more reactive so prices respond to shortages, wars, contracts, and player behavior.
7. Turn the castle into a stronger governance layer with meaningful council tradeoffs, edict side effects, and room synergies.
8. Add events, rivals, and scenario goals to create pressure and long-term direction beyond raw growth.

## Suggested Priority

1. Territory identity and conquest depth.
2. Population pressure and happiness.
3. Market reactivity and stronger trade contracts.
4. Better sinks for refined goods.
5. Castle and council strategic paths.
