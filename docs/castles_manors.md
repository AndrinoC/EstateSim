## Castles and Manors
* Add new resource in the top bar (not a typical produced resource): *Prestige*
* **Castles** can be constructed by paying a set amount of gold, planks, and stone
* Initial construction of the castle takes 400 ticks. Upon completion, the player begins earning 0.5 Prestige per tick.
* After construction is completed, it unlocks the Rooms tab, which is where the player can construct a variety of rooms for bonuses.
	* **Throne Room**: Unlocks *Edicts* which can be paid for using Prestige and Gold to grant temporary buffs to a town population growth or any resource for which there exists a building + at least one worker working it. The player chooses which Edict to grant.
	* **Barracks**: Reduces the cost of recruiting new regiments.
	* **Council Chamber**: Unlocks *Councilmembers* which are randomly generated characters that add particular randomized buffs when they are hired into a particular position (Marshal, Steward, Chancellor). For example, a council member can be recruited as Chancellor which will trigger their associated Chancellor buff and make trad agreements with X territory more likely to succeed. Or, if he were recruited as Marshal he would reduce regiment food costs by a certain %. Or, if her were recruited as Steward he would increase monthly gold income by a certain %, etc.
